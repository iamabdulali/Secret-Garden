SecretGarden’s species

All our collections will be based on the same logic : Everyone can see the collection of the plant following the color of the background. Everyone can see the rarity of a plant following the color of the pot. You can find 4 levels of rarity in the SecretGarden :

- Common : grey pot

- Rare : Blue pot

- Epic : Purple pot

- Legendary : Orange pot

Special events & partnerships may bring special rarity in the future, unique release …

Collection 01 : Monstera & CO

The first secret garden NFT collection is based on the Monstera . This is a famous plant all around the world loved by people & influencers. Monstera & CO collection was created as a pilot collection. Our main goal is to build a community, have a real test of our rarity & pricing system. We want to gather as much information as possible to move on together and build solid foundations for the future of SecretGarden.

Basic design of the collection

Every design is unique and handmade by a graphic designer. The point was to make a simple design, easily recognizable so it can find it’s place as a collectible NFT collection. You can find more information about the creative process in the « meet the gardener » section.

Every collection will have a specific background but the same rarity code with the pot’s color. The different details are here to highlight this rarity according to the real plant characteristics. Monstera Deliciosa is a tropical plant initially growing in Mexico & Guatemala in the tropical area. It’s one of the famous plant in the world. Some plants have a genetic mutation leading to a lack of chlorophyll and a white pigmentation of the leaves.

Two new species came from that. The Monstera Thai Constellation with spotting white pigmentation on the leaves and Monstera Albo with partial/complete white leaves. The whiter you have the rarest the plant is, and so is your NFT Details.

Marc, the first gardener

My name is Marc; I am 30 years old. I am passionate about the numeric world and new technologies. Still when I first heard about NFTs, I was skeptical because I had never taken the time to do substantial research on the matter. After a lot of digging into the details of this new universe, I found myself looking around the OpenSea marketplace, screening new NFT projects. Just like crypto currencies, it was more of a way to stay in touch with something huge for the future: something I found hugely interesting.

The trigger

During one of my digging sessions, I was on a call with my girlfriend trying to explain to her how impressed I was in my discoveries of this brand-new world. I have to tell you that her big passion is for plants. We have a lot of those in our apartment and I tried to show her the diversity in NFTs by trying to look for plant related NFTs. I was quite surprised to find a ridiculous amount of those in Opensea. I tried to go on different marketplaces … it was the same. Without my noticing it, the seed was planted… I started a new job in mid-2021. I was working abroad and I had a lot of spare time. The idea of creating a collection had been in the back of my head; I started to work relentlessly on the project. After spending a lot of time on the different marketplaces, I realized that a huge percentage of collections were very similar. Different layers with different backgrounds: the random generation of NFTs etc… but I wanted to do something unique.

The beginning

Spending time with my girlfriend sometimes means spending time in plant markets. I see a lot of diversity, a lot of beauty, in those spaces and I wanted to funnel this admiration into an NFT collection. I began to draw on my own, and quickly realized I needed someone to work with me on this. After quite some time, we narrowed our selection of plants down to 100, and that was the end of our first draft for the collection. The idea was to build our collection around a single type of plant, the Monstera Deliciosa. The Monstera Deliciosa is famous across both the plant world and the internet. It also happens to be my girlfriend’s favorite plant.

Ambitions

I really want to develop more than just a single collection. I want to develop something that matters on the long term. I want to show the diversity of this beautiful world with multiple collections. My goal is to highlight my fascination for it with a beautiful design, and also to explore my fundamental love of geek universe through the rarity process.

The concept

I am not a fan of the random generation, but I love the rarity concept. I’ve also been a fan of trading card games and item customization in video games. I wanted to bring that into my collection. I decided to put the rarity color code found in many video games (grey=common, blue=rare, purple=epic, orange=legendary) into the pot color of the plant. As such, anyone can easily see the rarity of each item inside the collection. I spent a lot of time thinking about the collection: the social presence, the marketing, and I’ve decided to surround myself with people to move in a direction I love.

Entering the Secret Garden

Since starting this project, I have found a lot of information saying that it’s not about the art: it’s about creating an investment opportunity, which I absolutely see. The approach I’ve taken is to not only make something that looks smooth and natural, but something which also serves as an investment. Whether you are a plant lover or an NFT lover, I really hope that you’ll find your place somewhere within my words and my ideas. I’m just a simple passionate guy who wants to share and enjoy with people. For the first time you have the chance to enter a brand new promising project not just as a buyer but as a part-owner of it. This collection is just the beginning and I really hope you’ll be there with me to write the following chapters of this incredible story.

The harvesting plan

My main goal is to build a strong and caring community. I want SecretGarden to be special: not just through its design or the story of the plants’ conception, but also through the relationship found within the community itself. If you have read my story, you already know that I created SecretGarden for the most part on my own, with some help here and there along the way. Now, I truly believe that we can build something powerful together. We can collectively write the future of SecretGarden NFT.

I want people to believe in the project, to believe in me. I have ambitious goals for the long run, and I know how hard it can be to stick with a project and to support it, especially when the creator does not have a long-standing history with NFTs. That is why I am creating a special Giveaway scheme, the Harvesting Plan. The plan is based on a cashback giveaway program.

Each holder will have future rewards, including whitelist presale access on the extension and on the following collection, and special roles on our social media and in our community.

But the main goal of the program is to give 40% of earnings back to the holders & to the community according to the following plan:

I) 20% divided by all the holders

II) 10% to active community members involved in the growth of social medias (ranking, inviting, etc…)

III) 10% to community members through social medias

I) Holders’ distributed revenue

Every holder holding an NFT for two weeks will receive several tickets according to the rarity of the NFT.

Common NFT (grey pot) = 1 ticket

Rare NFT (blue pot) = 2.5 tickets

Epic NFT (purple pot) = 4 tickets

Legendary NFT (orange pot) = 5 tickets

Every last Sunday of the month, the total earnings of the month’s sales will be divided by the number of tickets held. It will then be distributed as Wrapped ETH on the polygon network. The more NFTs you have, the more money you receive; the rarer your NFT, the more money you receive

II) Active members’ giveaway

Every two weeks, 10% of the earnings from sales will be distributed to 4 people as Wrapped ETH on the polygon network through a special channel in the discord server, reserved for the most active members on both twitter & discord community. A custom-designed bot (discord) and a giveaway website (twitter) will moderate the contest directly on those social medias.

III) Community giveaway

Every two weeks 10%, the earnings from sales will be distributed to 4 people as Wrapped ETH on the polygon network through the main giveaway channel in the discord server. A custom-designed bot will moderate the contest directly on the discord channel.

Note : Part I of the harvesting plan will remain the same for the first collection and the extensions of the first collection, according to our roadmap milestones. Part II and III of the harvesting plan will evolve according to the roadmap milestones, as follows:

Stage 1: Starts with the public sale opening: Every 2 weeks, 0.005 WETH is given to 4 gardeners

Stage 2: Starts at 25% sales milestone: Every 2 weeks, 0.015 WETH is given to 4 gardeners

Stage 3: Starts at 50% sales milestone: Every 2 weeks, 0.03 WETH is given to 4 gardeners

Stage 4: Starts at 75% sales milestone: Every 2 weeks 0.045 WETH is given to 4 gardeners

Final stage: Starts at 100% sales milestone: Every 2 weeks 0.060 WETH is given to 4 gardeners
